About

I'm Badara Aliou Guindo, a Data Science & AI Master's student at the Faculté des Sciences de Meknès, Morocco. Originally from Mali, I bring curiosity, rigour, and a diverse perspective to every technical challenge.

My approach is pragmatic: I don't build models for the sake of it. Every project targets a concrete problem — detecting a disease, predicting consumption, understanding sentiment. I go all the way through, from data exploration to a production-ready application.

I'm fluent in the full Data Science spectrum: classical ML with interpretability via SHAP, Deep Learning across CNN, LSTM, U-Net and Transformer architectures, multilingual NLP, and medical computer vision. I am open to all opportunities — research, industry, freelance — wherever I can create meaningful impact.
