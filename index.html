About me

I'm Badara Aliou Guindo, a Data Science & AI Master's student at Faculté des Sciences de Meknès, Morocco. Originally from Mali, I bring a diverse perspective to every project I work on.

My work focuses on building end-to-end machine learning systems — from raw data exploration to deployed web applications. I believe in making models not only accurate, but explainable and actionable.

I've worked on projects spanning medical imaging, NLP, time series forecasting, and computer vision — always with a focus on real-world impact and clean, reproducible code.
